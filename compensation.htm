Benefits and Compensation

Compensation

Class A drivers $19-$20 hourly
Class B drivers $18-$19 hourly

Benefits

HDL has a competitive benefit plan for our CDL drivers.

Paid Holidays - Drivers earn six (6) paid holidays per calendar year, following 3 months of full time (30 hrs/wk) employment. Employees are required to be available for work on the days prior to and immediately following the holiday.

Vacation - Drivers earn one week (calculated based on the average number of hours worked per week over 52 week) vacation at the end of the first year of full time (30 hours/week) employment. This increases to two (2) weeks after 3 years of Full time (30 hour/week) employment.

Insurance Offerings

HDL sponsors an employee benefit package that has been designed for both quality and ease of use. Drivers are eligible for HDL's, reimbursed insurance after 90 days of working 30 hours.

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield health insurance
  • United Health Care dental plan
