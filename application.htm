Truck drivers with a 'can do' attitude and a proven track record are always welcome.

Highway Driver Leasing (HDL) is constantly on the lookout for drivers who have a minimum of 2 years of recent, verifiable CDL A or B experience, good references, a clean driving record and a can do attitude. If you want to be well compensated for your hard work—please let us know what we can do for you.

Call us at 800.332.6620 or fill in the form below.

We assure you, HDL will not inundate you with emails. All information you provide to HDL is kept private. We NEVER share or rent our contact lists.

To apply to Highway Driver Leasing you MUST:

  • Be 23 years of age or older
  • Willing to take a drug test
  • Have a valid DOT Med Card
  • Have at least two years of verifiable truck driving experience
