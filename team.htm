HDL Team

Linda Greenberg, President

lgreenberg@highwaydriverleasing.com

Connecting people to opportunities is in Linda's DNA. She brings more than thirty-years of truck driver safety, DOT compliance, business management and human resource experience to her position at Highway Driver Leasing. She is HDL's key strategist who is responsible for quality control and the day-to-day business leadership. Linda joined HDL in 1989 as a driver recruiter and worked her way up through the organization. She is a graduate of Boston University.

 

Wendy MacPherson, Safety Director

wmacpherson@highwaydriverleasing.com

Wendy joined HDL in 1997 and has a proven know-how, a keen intuitive sense and practically a cult driver following. Wendy ensures compliance and consistency of all human resource and safety functions at HDL—including pre-hire review of all truck driver applicants and driver qualification file management. She designs and manages HDL's safety initiatives and heads our Safety Review Committee. Wendy has earned her Safety Director Certification from the North American Transportation Institute. Wendy is a graduate of Boston Latin School and attended Suffolk University.

 

Danielle Bollin, Operations Manager

dbollin@highwaydriverleasing.com

With management responsibility for HDL's 24/7 driver dispatch and processing functions, Danielle handles a variety of details from both HDL drivers and companies. She ensures that customers have the drivers they need and is a detailed and precise dispatcher. Danielle joined HDL in 2010 and is a graduate of Quincy College.

 

Lisa Ferraro, Driver Recruiter

lferraro@highwaydriverleasing.com

Lisa is the gatekeeper and nurturer of relationships with HDL drivers. She conducts preliminary truck driver interviews, checks references and is responsible for the initial screening of driver applicants.  She brings her experience working with detailed problem solving and has a keen eye for driver recruiting. Lisa is a graduate of Keene State College.
