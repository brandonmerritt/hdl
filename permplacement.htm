HDL will take this away from you...

  • Multiple help wanted listings
  • Bombardment of unqualified applicants
  • Numerous telephone screenings
  • Too many interviews
  • Endless background checks
  • No guarantee of finding your driver

HDL has truck drivers that you can't find elsewhere.

Most of our drivers are currently working full-time and are looking for other full-time opportunities. They tend to not be found via online listing or newspaper ads. Our drivers rely and trust that HDL will save them time by screening companies on their behalf. Ultimately, our clients will get a higher quality driver from HDL.

You will only interview candidates who you will want to hire.

We inquire with select drivers who meet or exceed your hiring criteria. We then choose our best candidate based on the right personality fit, skills and experience, and their interest level.

Drivers keep HDL on their radar.

Since 1982 we've cultivated a following of drivers who look to HDL for employment. Our truck drivers know that when they get a call from HDL, it is for a great opportunity. Our drivers know that we are not just picky about the drivers we hire, we are also selective about the clients we place them with.

Our 30-day guarantee.

HDL offers a 30-day guarantee for all full-time placements. If your driver does not meet your expectations, we will replace the driver free of charge.

Temporary-to-full-time placement is always an option.

Many of our clients are interested in hiring HDL's drivers on a temporary basis, before committing to full-time employment. Our "temp-to-perm" arrangement is the way to make the transition. Call us for information about this option.

HDL welcomes experienced truck drivers with clean driving records, good references, current DOT cards and a "can do" attitude. Apply here.
