Let HDL find the best job for you.

HDL has a 30-year track record of listening to our truck drivers' needs and matching each person with the best-known companies throughout New England. Whether you are looking for a temporary, part-time or full-time position, we will only tell you about opportunities that meet your personal job criteria.

Your safety is our top priority.

We are committed to placing our truck drivers ONLY in safe environments. None of our jobs have backbreaking lifting requirements. HDL's strict safety policies are based on our commitment to you coming home safe at the end of your day.

Drive locally and go home daily.

Our clients are located throughout Massachusetts, Connecticut, New Hampshire, Rhode Island, and Maine. We will only dispatch you close to home, and you can be home every night.

No Forced Dispatch.

HDL drivers are able to choose from a number of attractive companies and accept positions based on their personal choice.  There is no Forced Dispatch.

Flexibility and choice are yours.

We listen closely to find out what type of work you are looking for, where you want to work, and the hours of the day that are best for you, we then provide you with opportunities that meet your needs. We let you know the important details of each job, so you will be able to make the right decision.

Integrity is vital to our success.

Our temporary drivers have worked for HDL for an average of eight years. We value integrity, honesty and respect, above all else.
